FAQ

Any questions? Look here!

What is the purpose of this Virtual Herbal Garden?

The garden is an educational resource to explore medicinal plants used in AYUSH systems (Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy).

Are the plants shown here real and safe to use?

The plants are based on real medicinal species. However, this site is for learning only. Always consult certified practitioners before using herbs for treatment.

Can I contribute or suggest more plants?

Yes! This prototype can be expanded. You can add plants by editing the plants.json (or array in script.js).

Does this site work offline?

Yes, since it is a static site. Just download the files and open index.html in your browser.

Will there be multilingual support?

Currently, it’s in English. Future versions can include Hindi, Tamil, and other languages.
